Project Architecture Deep Dives

HearWise Child Health

Role: Lead Architect & Full-Stack Developer

ReactTypeScriptTailwindSupabase

The Systemic Problem

Childhood hearing loss is a massive, underdiagnosed crisis, particularly within resource-constrained government and rural school systems. Traditional audiological screening requires highly trained specialists and capital-intensive hardware. Consequently, millions of children suffer from delayed detection, cascading into irreversible deficits in speech and cognitive development.

The Engineered Solution

I architected HearWise, a decentralized, mobile-first digital hearing screening platform. It shifts the primary diagnostic capability from the hospital directly into the classroom, empowering non-specialist educators to conduct diagnostically valid preliminary screenings in under 10 minutes per subject.

Technical Highlights & Clinical Validation

  • Offline-First Resilience: Engineered with a robust asynchronous offline data queue via browser storage APIs to combat network instability.
  • Clinical Safety Protocols: Integrated hardware readiness gates adhering to WHO and AAA guidelines to minimize false positives.
  • Audiometric Precision: Delivers precise four-frequency sweeps (500 Hz, 1000 Hz, 2000 Hz, 4000 Hz) via standard headphones.
  • Enterprise Admin Dashboard: Features React-Leaflet geographical mapping, date filtering, and strict IP/Device audit logging.
  • Bilingual Accessibility: Natively supports English and Tamil for localized adoption.

Yudhisthra (TrafficVision AI)

Role: AI & Machine Learning Engineer

PythonYOLOv8Computer VisionRL

The Systemic Problem

Modern urban infrastructure relies heavily on fixed-timer traffic lights that are blind to actual road conditions. This static approach leads to severe urban gridlock, wasted fuel, and critically, delayed emergency response times when ambulances are trapped behind unmoving traffic.

The Engineered Solution

Yudhisthra is an Intelligent Traffic Management System designed to serve as the "eyes and brain" of smart city intersections. It replaces static timers with dynamic, vision-based adaptive control algorithms that process live physical reality.

Technical Highlights & System Modules

  • The Vision Module: Utilizes YOLOv8 models to process live camera feeds, calculating precise vehicle density and lane congestion in real-time.
  • The Logic Module: Adaptive algorithms process density data to adjust light durations instantaneously, preemptively reallocating time from empty lanes to congested ones.
  • Emergency Override Pipeline: Models are trained to classify emergency vehicles, triggering an immediate signal override protocol to clear routes without delay.
